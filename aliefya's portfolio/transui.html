TransUI

Project / Website App Design + Front-End Development

DURATION: May 2023 - Present

COLLABORATOR: Rezki Muhammad, Leonardo Jeremy

LINK: TransUI (in-progress)

The application aims to provide information about various modes of public transportation within and to UI. It offers services for ordering ojek (motorcycle taxi), renting spekun (bicycles), and monitoring bikun (beam) and KRL (train) schedules. The system consists of seven databases, namely User, Mitra Ojek, Jadwal Bikun, List Spekun, List KRL, Database Admin, and Database Wallet. It caters to three types of users: regular users (consumers), ojek partners (drivers), and administrators. Users and admins have access to a wallet for transactions.
